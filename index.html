A Premium Experience for Solo Travelers

The perfect haven for the solo traveler, GoSolo Stays our premium boutique hostel gives you comfort, connivence and the company of like-minded travelers in the heart of Mumbai.

A walk away from all the city hot spots but away from the bustle, the quaint century old bungalow makes you fall in love with its old-world charm. Our well-trained staff available 24X7 and stringent hygiene standards make your stay stress-free and memorable.

Premium comfortable beds, full-service amenities, and facilities to make work and play a breeze. Enjoy our complimentary breakfast as you bond with people from around world and start off on your Mumbai adventure.
