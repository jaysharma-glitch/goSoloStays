Mixed Dormitory

Sink into our comfortable beds after a day exploring or when back from work. Premium linen, high quality mattress, designed for privacy our bunk beds offer you the best of both worlds. Spend your time chatting with like-minded travelers or use our in-bed laptop tables to catch up on work.

We understand the needs of a solo female traveler and offer an exclusive female only dorm with an in-suite washroom, full length mirrors, easy to access bunks with premium linen. We are committed to offer the highest standards of cleanliness and safety for our women guests.
